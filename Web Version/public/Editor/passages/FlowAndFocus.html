Flow and Focus
Flow is a state of mind that can be achieved with great concentration and focus
Flow state is the most productive state we can be in.
Removing two froms of distractions
One NN External Distractions
Social media, phone, notifications, and noise. These are external distractions
Two NN Internal Distractions
thoughts, emotions stress and mind racing. These are internal distractions
The point is to do meaningful deep work, without distractions.
Identify the biggest external distractions that hijack you.
You must either remove these external distractions or remove yourself from these external distractions.
The harder it is to access distractions, the less likely youll get distracted.
If distractions are easily accessible, you will get distracted.
These external distractions are designed to get you hooked on them. They are unnatrually stimulating to the mind.
Your brain releases a lot of dopamine while engaged in these distractions which is why it is so difficult to resist their temptation
The mind is always looking for the easiest way to get stimulated the most.
Most of our daily tasks cannot beat the instant gratification that these distractions provide.
You must remove and deprive yourself of all distractions until your work is the only fun and stimulating thing to do.
When there are no distractions you create the space to enter flow state and do deep meaningful work.
After removing external distractions, the mind is the only big source of distractions left.
You must learn how to calm your mind and limit the internal chatter.
Meditation is one of the most effective ways to remove the internal chatter and distractions
Read our passages and study about meditation
Monkey Mind
Monkey Mind is when your thoughts are swinging from branch to branch. You cannot focus on anything because you choose to indulge in every thought that entertains or stimulates you. Thus inhibiting your flow and focus
We can elliminate Monkey Mind with meditation.
Read and study about meditation techniques and realize the great benefit it has on the mind. Then, meditate
Another way we can elliminate Monkey Mind is by journaling our thoughts on paper. This allows us to see our thoughts as a whole and to be able to digest them without distraction.
Daily journaling helps a lot to capture thoughts, free up mental resources and it protects your mental energy.
Your mind shouldnt be a storage unit for thoughts and ideas. Instead it should be a generator of high quality ideas.
Taming the monkey mind sharpens your focus allowing for deep work
Now Do it! Knowing how is not enough. We must put these thoughts and knowing into practice and action.
