The Seven Laws of Tehuti
NN One NN
Mentalism
All is Mind.
The Universe is Mental.
NN Two NN
Correspondence
As above, so below.
As within, so without.
NN Three NN
Vibration
Nothing rests.
Everything moves.
Everything vibrates.
NN Four NN
Polarity
Everything is Dual.
Everything has Poles.
Everything has its pair of opposites.
Like and alike are the same.
NN Five NN
Rhythm
Everything flows;
out and in.
Everything has its tides.
All things rise and fall.
NN Six NN
Cause and Effect
Every cause has its effect.
Every effect has its cause.
NN Seven NN
Gender
Gender is in everything
Everything has its masculine and feminine principles.
Gender manifests on all planes.
